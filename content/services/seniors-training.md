# :older_adult: IT Training for Seniors
**Technology Made Simple and Accessible**

## Empowering Seniors with Technology Skills

I provide patient, understanding IT training specifically designed for older adults. My approach focuses on practical skills, safety, and building confidence with modern technology at a comfortable pace.

## Why Technology Training for Seniors?

**:family: Stay Connected**  
Keep in touch with family and friends through email, video calls, and social media.

**:shield: Stay Safe Online**  
Learn to recognize and avoid scams, protect personal information, and browse safely.

**:convenience_store: Daily Convenience**  
Online banking, shopping, appointment booking, and accessing services from home.

**:brain: Mental Stimulation**  
Learning new skills keeps the mind active and engaged.

## Training Programs

### :computer: Computer Basics
**Perfect for absolute beginners**

- Understanding computer components
- Using mouse and keyboard effectively
- File management and organization
- Basic software navigation
- Printing and scanning documents

### :globe_with_meridians: Internet & Email
**Connecting with the digital world**

- Safe web browsing techniques
- Creating and managing email accounts
- Sending attachments and photos
- Online search strategies
- Recognizing and avoiding scams

### :iphone: Smartphones & Tablets
**Mobile technology made simple**

- Basic device operation
- Making calls and sending texts
- Installing and using apps
- Taking and sharing photos
- Voice assistants and accessibility features

### :credit_card: Online Banking & Shopping
**Secure digital transactions**

- Setting up online banking safely
- Understanding security features
- Online shopping best practices
- Password management
- Protecting financial information

### :busts_in_silhouette: Social Media & Communication
**Staying connected with loved ones**

- Facebook, WhatsApp, and other platforms
- Video calling (Zoom, Skype, FaceTime)
- Sharing photos and memories
- Privacy settings and safety
- Digital etiquette

## Teaching Approach

### :snail: Patient Pace
**No rushing, no pressure**  
We move at your speed, with plenty of time to practice and ask questions.

### :repeat: Repetition Welcome
**Practice makes perfect**  
We'll review concepts as many times as needed until you feel confident.

### :memo: Written Materials
**Take-home guides**  
Clear, large-print instructions you can reference at home.

### :handshake: One-on-One Attention
**Personalized instruction**  
Individual or small group sessions tailored to your specific needs.

### :house: Comfortable Environment
**Learn where you're comfortable**  
Sessions available in your home or in a relaxed, friendly setting.
