About

Calm engineering, practical communication.

I am based in Vienna and work around cloud-native infrastructure, automation, and developer-facing operations. My strongest work usually sits between platform engineering and production support: reducing friction while keeping the system understandable.

I value small changes, documented decisions, and tools that can be debugged by the people who depend on them. The goal is not to make infrastructure impressive; it is to make it trustworthy.
